GAD-7 (Generalized Anxiety Disorder Scale)

Spitzer, R. L., Kroenke, K., Williams, J. B. W., & Löwe, B. (2006). A brief measure for assessing generalized anxiety disorder: The GAD-7. Archives of Internal Medicine, 166(10), 1092-1097.

DOI: 10.1001/archinte.166.10.1092

This is a preview only

Your responses aren't saved or sent anywhere. The page below renders the questionnaire with the same templates a participant would see inside a BOFS experiment. Click Compute results at the bottom to see the raw values BOFS would store plus any participant calculations the questionnaire defines.

Over the last 2 weeks, how often have you been bothered by the following problems?
Not at all Several days More than half the days Nearly every day
Feeling nervous, anxious, or on edge
Not being able to stop or control worrying
Worrying too much about different things
Trouble relaxing
Being so restless that it is hard to sit still
Becoming easily annoyed or irritable
Feeling afraid, as if something awful might happen
If you checked any problems, how difficult have they made it for you to do your work, take care of things at home, or get along with other people?
